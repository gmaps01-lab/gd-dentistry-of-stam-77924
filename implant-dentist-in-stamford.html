Frequently Asked Questions About implant dentist

How much do dental implants typically cost at GD Dentistry of Stamford, and does insurance cover any of the procedure?
At GD Dentistry of Stamford, the cost for a single dental implant typically ranges from $3,000 to $4,500, depending on the complexity of your case and the type of restoration needed. While some dental insurance plans in Connecticut may partially cover aspects of the procedure (such as the crown or extraction), the implant itself is often considered a cosmetic procedure and may not be fully covered. We recommend bringing your insurance information to your consultation so our team can provide a detailed estimate and help you understand your coverage options.
How long does the dental implant process take from start to finish at your Stamford office?
The dental implant process at our Stamford office generally takes between 3 to 6 months from your initial consultation to the placement of the final crown. This timeline can vary depending on your individual needs and whether you require preparatory procedures like bone grafting. After the implant is placed, a healing period of several months is needed to allow the bone to fuse with the implant before the permanent crown is attached. We’ll provide a personalized treatment plan and timeline during your first visit.
Are your implant dentists licensed and experienced with dental implants?
Yes, all implant procedures at GD Dentistry of Stamford are performed by licensed dentists who have completed extensive training in implant dentistry. Our team regularly attends continuing education courses and uses the latest technology to ensure safe and successful outcomes. We are fully licensed to practice in Connecticut and are members of professional organizations such as the American Dental Association and the Connecticut State Dental Association.
What is the step-by-step process for getting a dental implant at GD Dentistry of Stamford?
The dental implant process at our Stamford office starts with a comprehensive consultation, including digital X-rays and 3D imaging to assess your bone structure. If you’re a candidate, we’ll schedule the surgical placement of the implant post under local anesthesia. After a healing period of several months (to allow the implant to integrate with the bone), you’ll return for placement of an abutment and then a custom-made crown. Throughout the process, our team will guide you and answer any questions you may have.
Do you offer any warranties or guarantees on dental implants provided at your Stamford practice?
We stand behind the quality of our dental implants and materials. At GD Dentistry of Stamford, we offer a limited warranty on our implant crowns and abutments for up to five years, provided patients maintain recommended oral hygiene and attend regular check-ups. If you experience any issues with your implant, please contact our office right away so we can evaluate and address your concerns. We are committed to your long-term oral health and satisfaction.
